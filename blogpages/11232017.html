Over the Rainbow

November 23rd, 2017

Slept like garbage the night before so getting up was a bit more difficult than I would've liked this morning. Plus, I think my body was not looking forward to running another 3 miles after getting up to 5.25 the night before. I've been at this plateau for a few months where I haven't been able to break the 4.5 mark on my runs. For some reason, I broke that last night and felt amazing afterwards. I think it's a direct correlation to the amount of eggnog being consumed. Regardless the reason, I needed to earn that Apple acheivement badge for Turkey Day. It's like Pokémon only healthier! Ran down to the Grand Army Plaza, looped around, and headed home. 3.2 miles and badge earned. Soak in all its digital glory!

Helped Danielle prep the downstairs AirBnB for some new guests coming and then spent the rest of the late morning/early afternoon joking with Sarah and Lauren while we all prepped our chosen potluck dishes. Lauren cooked an incredible turkey, Sarah made some delcious and creamy mashed potatoes, and I made a couple of tins of green bean casserole. I think the shallots, worchestershire, and garlic I threw into the mix helped make them pop. Plenty of eggnog and bourbon to be had (I thought about us, Dad) and eventually our friends started to arrive.

TL:DR

Food and shelter, happy and content.

“Somewhere over the rainbow, Skies are blue. And the dreams that you dare to dream really do come true ” — Yip Harburg

Copious amounts of wine and a turkey dinner fit for royalty was presented around 6pm. Only a few hours after we thought we were to eat but I guess good things come to those who wait and this was no exception to that. I'm always eating way too much and I didn't disappoint myself this year either. One of these Thanksgiving days I will actually commit to having one nice sized plate and call it.

After a laugh filled evening, most everyone left or went to bed. Lauren and I watched a few episodes of a couple of different shows before she finally called a Lyft and took her and Sarah back to Bed-Stuy. I spent another hour cleaning up downstairs before finally trudging upstairs and passing out. Overall, an amazing day and it is only one of the many things I was so grateful and thankful for. Happy Thanksgiving everyone!
