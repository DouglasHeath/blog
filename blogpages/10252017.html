Empire State Of Mind

October 25th, 2017

8AM came REALLY early for some reason. Maybe it's the jet lag? Pacific Standard Time affinity? Bah. Nothing some delicious pour over can't remedy (thanks again, Sarah!). After a wonderful evening with Sarah, Lauren, and Danielle, we retired to Bed-Stuy for some good ol' Cage Rage therapy. Last night's classic? The Rock. Unfortunately Lauren and I forgot how much paperwork and bureaucracy precedes the EPIC Alcatraz showdown, much to Sarah's chagrin. Sarah's also convinced that Michael Bay purposely wrote as many 's' words he could just so Sean Connery could speak with that SHHHcotish ACSHHHent.

Headed into Manhattan today for two first step meetings with a pair of marketing firms. What do I know about marketing? ZIP. But I am good with people so maybe that translates? First interview was 'meh'. Second was much more relaxed although they both followed that East Coast format; FAST and FURIOUS. Seriously, 3 minutes each. At least I ate an overpriced spice chicken wrap.

Rode the subway back to S and L's place to check in on Yams (their sweet kitty) and to take off the monkey suit. I should hear back from both companies tonight whether or not I make the cut. Maybe grab a drink with Karen? Regardless, first day going well, don't worry about me, mom! ðŸ˜‰
