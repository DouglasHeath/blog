We Said Hello Goodbye

November 11th, 2017

FIRST WEEK DOWN! Working 40 hours that is. There were some real snoozers of days during this past week for sure. There were also some great laughs and a chance to meet some new people both to me and to New York. In fact, one of them reminded me that I should think about getting some new cards made up to hand out. ü§î

Its now Saturday morning and yo're probably thinking "What HAPPENED in the last 5 days!?" Well friends, sadly nothing truly worth noting. Life as normal. Which begs the question; do I update this thing daily no matter how mundane and trivial my experiences or do I wait to only post things when they have a larger impact? I can't believe you'd want to read "walked to the store, got some pasta sauce, the end" every day. I mean, I don't get pasta sauce every day so I'd think it'd be a tad more thrilling than that but honestly after the week I just experienced, there really isn't much more I could write.

I can say that there is a recurring theme; I don't seem to get much more than 5 hours of sleep each night. For the last two weeks I've been here. I blame a combination of too much coffee in the afternoon, a bit too much alchohol too late in the evening, and the fact that I am living in the city that never sleeps. I think there is this underlying "pulse" here in New York that I'm picking up subconciously while sleeping. It isn't like I wake up at 4:30AM all groggy and confused rather, I wake up at 4:30AM CHARGED UP and ready to tackle the day.

Okay, I did sleep in this morning (it is 7:24AM as of right this moment) and I think a lot of it had to do with the loss of having to make sure I had time to get ready for my day at the office. I can't believe I just wrote that; my day at the office. üòÇ Knowing that my day is wide open led to me finally enjoying a full 7 hours. And I feel GREAT! Today's agenda includes having a run to the park since Apple's giving out a virtual achievement for working out on Veteran's Day. And you are welcome and I also thank all of YOU who served.

Sounds like Danielle and I might be hanging out with her friends later this evening. Possible dancing? I dunno. Brunch tomorrow with S and L. Then meeting up with Andrea since she's throwing out some preposterous claim that she might have procured a pony for me. Yeah, I thought the SAME thing so of COURSE I have to see what this girl's up to. Considering she went to MIT I think this might be one of the best pranks I've ever been a victim of. I did tell her I'd probably board a pony down in Prospect Park but with the weather turning, that'd just be animal cruelty.

Oh, forgot to mention the weather. While I don't have snow *cough* Kristyn and Pink Esper *cough* you will all be happy to know that the weather went from like 58 to 31 literally overnight. I'm so glad that I brought my smart wool base layers. Next will be some leather gloves.

BIG Happy Birthdays to my sister Kristyn and my brother Josh. Kristyn finally made it into the 40's club. WAIT, I MEAN Kristyn will NEVER make it into the 40's club. I love you both and am glad to have you in my life through all of the ups and downs. With ALL of us, AMIRITE!? üéÅüéàüéÇ
