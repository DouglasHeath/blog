Green Belly

November 1st, 2017

Time is flying fast! Guess that's part of the downfall of getting older but I'm doing my best to keep on top of it and I'd say I'm doing better than I've ever done. That's not why you're here though, is it? You want to know "WHAT DID YOU DO FOR HALLOWEEN!?

Well, Lindsay said she'd rather go to Emily than Speedy Romeo. So I trained it over to Clinton and Fulton to hit up Emily and see what all the hubbub was about. Tiny, narrow place with lots of delicious aromas and eclectic playlist action. Great bartender (one of the best Negronis I've EVER tasted) and super friendly staff all over the joint. Seriously, anyone who laughs at MY silly sense of humor is a winner. Lindsay and I shared a caesar and the Colony, which is a pepperoni pizza with pickled jalapeńos and a drizzle of honey. The combo sweet and spicy put Emily into the top contender for "Best Pizza Yet" award here in Brooklyn. Which is not to be confused with "Best Pizza Ever" award which knows no geographical boundary and will probably never be awarded. Although Stellar is leading at the moment.

Headed into Manhattan to do some temp work for a company an old friend hooked me up with. Yeah, I'm pulling the Melanie Griffith "Working Girl" right now but sans EPIC curly blonde hair and late 80's business causal skirts. Spent the afternoon cold-calling potential clients to convince them to particiapte in a research project. Which OF COURSE made every call an exercise in trying to convince the party that I wasn't trying to scam or sell them anything. I'm the oldest guy in the group but probably the most confident (EGO!!). Regardless, made some great connections and had an EPIC view of Manhattan from the offices on the 43rd floor. Scroll back to the top if you need a reminder. I'll be doing that tomorrow, Friday, and again on Monday. It's peanuts compared to what I need to make in order to survive, but at least its coming.

Its getting dark early, as it is everywhere. Which means my runs are either going to have to be SUPER early or weekends exclusive. At least for the next few days. I don't think I'm quite ready to run around Prospect in the dark.
